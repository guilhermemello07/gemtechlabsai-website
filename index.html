FoodLens

Know what you eat

Scan any food product barcode and instantly get an AI-powered health analysis. FoodLens decodes ingredient lists, identifies harmful additives, and gives you a clear health score — so you can make informed choices at the grocery store.

  • Instant Scan

    Point your camera at any barcode for real-time analysis

  • Ingredient Analysis

    AI-powered breakdown of every ingredient and additive

  • Health Score

    Clear 0-100 score based on nutrition, additives, and processing

  • Risk Alerts

    Warnings about harmful additives and allergens
